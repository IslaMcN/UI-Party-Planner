Having a small office Christmas party? Maybe use lights in jars or candles if your work allows it. Have a table to put out snacks and drinks. Make it quaint, quiet and a little romantic.

Having a birthday party for your little one? Or hey, maybe for you? Consider using a rustic vibe with a painted board with text to fit your parties theme. Unicorns are big right now so you cannot go wrong with a unicorn theme.

Having a celebration? Colorful pennants can really liven up the atmosphere.

Let's face it, disco never died. Have a disco ball or ten at your next big party. Dance the night away with all that amazing reflected light!

Celebrating an aniversary or maybe just having an intimate gathering? Candles in jars. Trust us. It is romantic and cute.

Look at all this light! It's so open and the way the big plant to the left juxtaposes the thin and delicate arch. Make a STATEMENT at your wedding.

Color themes are important. The way this all set up looks clean and inviting. Having a charger for the plate to sit on (it's that little woven circle under the flatware) will look elegant. No matter the occasion, this kind of dining is a MUST.

Having friends over to watch the game? Go with having a spread of snacks. Have your guests just dig in. This is how they party in Italy so why not!

Little twinkly lights are adorable and they also provide, well, light! They go great with any party including birthdays and weddings.

Lastly, have sparklers at your next evening party. Not enough people do this. It is fun and exciting. Just don't burn your house down. Can you even burn anyting with a sparkler? We don't know but just do it and be careful.
